Korean name: 마디 촌

Meaning: a unit of measurement for length, small, few

Examples:
* 촌수 (寸數) how many degrees of separation between family members <br><br>
  삼촌 (三寸) uncle, paternal uncle <br><br>
  사촌 (四寸) cousin <br><br>
* 촌극 (寸劇) a very short play, performance <br><br>
